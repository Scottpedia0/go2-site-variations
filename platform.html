What Go2 captures

Better inputs create better AI

Most systems capture fragments. Go2 captures the workflow.

Total capture — app usage, browser activity, workflow sequences, keystrokes, audio transcription, idle detection — is what makes Go2's AI specific rather than generic. It sees the complete picture of how work actually happens, not a partial view filtered through self-reporting or sampling.

This is the core difference. Competitors capture less, which means their AI knows less. Less context produces generic recommendations that people ignore. Full context produces coaching that feels like it was written by someone who sat next to you all week.

⚡ App & browser activity

Every application, every tab, every switch — mapped into workflow sequences.

⌨ Keystroke patterns

Typing cadence and volume reveal focus, struggle, and repetitive data entry.

🎙 Audio transcription

Meeting and call context feeds the AI — so coaching accounts for verbal workflows too.

⏳ Idle detection

Distinguishes deep thought from disengagement. Context matters.
