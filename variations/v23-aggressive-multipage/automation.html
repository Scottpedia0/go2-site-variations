The Automation Pipeline

From observation to autonomy

Every automation starts with understanding. Go2's pipeline moves from passive observation to active execution in five stages.

1

Map human workflows

AI observes how tasks actually flow through your organization. Not how you think they work — how they actually work.

2

Identify repetitive patterns

Flag tasks that follow consistent rules: same inputs, same steps, same outputs. These are automation candidates.

3

Build agent playbooks

Generate step-by-step agent instructions from observed human behavior. The playbook IS the workflow, codified.

4

Deploy agents

AI agents execute playbooks autonomously. Human-in-the-loop for edge cases. Full audit trail for every action.

5

Humans do higher-value work

Freed from repetitive tasks, your team focuses on strategy, creative work, and relationship building.

What Agents Handle

Real work. Fully automated.

These aren't hypothetical. These are the types of workflows Go2 identifies and automates from real observational data.

Data Entry

Invoice processing

Agent extracts invoice data, matches to POs, validates amounts, routes for approval, and enters into accounting system. Handles 90%+ of invoices without human touch.

Saves ~15 hrs/week per AP clerk
Reporting

Weekly status reports

Agent pulls data from project management tools, formats updates by team, highlights blockers and risks, distributes to stakeholders. Every Monday at 8am. Zero human effort.

Saves ~4 hrs/week per PM
HR Operations

Onboarding workflows

Agent creates accounts, assigns training modules, schedules orientation meetings, provisions equipment requests, and sends day-1 welcome packets. Triggered by offer acceptance.

Saves ~8 hrs per new hire
Customer Support

Ticket triage and routing

Agent classifies incoming tickets, checks knowledge base for existing solutions, auto-responds to known issues, routes complex cases to the right specialist with full context attached.

Saves ~20 hrs/week per support team
Sales Operations

CRM hygiene and follow-ups

Agent updates deal stages based on email activity, schedules follow-up tasks, enriches contact records from public sources, flags stale opportunities for review.

Saves ~6 hrs/week per rep
Compliance

Audit preparation

Agent collects required documentation across systems, organizes by audit category, identifies gaps, generates compliance checklists, and pre-fills audit workpapers.

Saves ~40 hrs per audit cycle
