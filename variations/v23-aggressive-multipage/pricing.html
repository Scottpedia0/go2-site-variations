14 days, full product, no credit card required. You'll see coaching nudges within 48 hours of deployment. Workflow maps start generating by week 2.

What's the minimum team size?

No minimum. Go2 works for teams of 5 and teams of 5,000. The AI coaching works at any scale — the team dashboards become more powerful with more data.

How long does deployment take?

Under an hour. Lightweight agent installation, SSO configuration if needed, and you're live. No IT overhaul. No multi-week implementation.

Can workers opt out of coaching?

Workers control their notification preferences. They can adjust frequency, timing, and topic areas. The AI adapts to preferences over time. Data capture continues for team-level insights.

What about data security?

SOC 2 Type II certified. Data encrypted at rest and in transit. Enterprise customers can self-host. We process patterns, not content. Full data residency controls available.

Do managers really only see aggregates?

Yes. This is architecturally enforced, not policy-enforced. The manager view physically cannot show individual worker data. This isn't a toggle — it's how the system is built.

What's included in AI agent deployment?

Business plan includes automated playbook generation, agent deployment across your tool stack, human-in-the-loop controls, and a full audit trail. You pick which workflows to automate.
