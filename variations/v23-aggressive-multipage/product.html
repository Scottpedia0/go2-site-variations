Privacy Architecture

Capture everything. Expose nothing.

Go2 is built on a privacy-first architecture. The AI needs full context to coach effectively. Managers never need individual surveillance to lead effectively.

Workers own their data

Every worker can see exactly what Go2 captures about them. Full transparency. No hidden monitoring. Workers control notification preferences and coaching frequency.

Managers see aggregates only

Team-level patterns, department-level trends, org-wide opportunities. Never individual timelines. Never "what was Sarah doing at 2:47pm." That's not management — it's surveillance.

Content-blind analysis

Go2 analyzes patterns, not content. We know you spent 22 minutes in a spreadsheet. We don't read the spreadsheet. We know you had a meeting. We don't record the meeting.
