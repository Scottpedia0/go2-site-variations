How it works

A lightweight agent that makes your team faster

Go2 captures how work actually happens, then uses frontier AI to coach every person individually. No behavior changes required. No complex setup.

What Go2 captures

A small desktop agent runs quietly on each team member's computer. It observes work patterns without interrupting them.

Application usage

Which tools your team uses, how long they spend in each, and how they move between them. Reveals hidden workflow bottlenecks.

Work patterns

When deep focus happens, where context-switching burns time, and how different people approach the same tasks.

Process flows

The actual steps people take to complete recurring work. Surfaces the difference between the "official" process and what really happens.

Activity context

Enough context to understand what's happening, without capturing personal content. Think metadata, not surveillance.

Go2 does not capture keystrokes, passwords, personal messages, or screen recordings. Workers control what's shared.

What the AI does with it

Raw activity data goes into frontier AI models — Claude, GPT, Gemini — which analyze patterns and generate actionable coaching.

1

Pattern recognition

AI identifies how each person works: their habits, their strengths, where they lose time. It builds an individual profile for every team member.

2

Peer benchmarking

Compares workflows across your team (and across our data from ~1M workers) to find what the fastest, most effective approaches look like.

3

Individual coaching

Delivers specific, personalized tips to each worker. Not generic advice — coaching based on what they actually do every day.

4

Automation mapping

Identifies repetitive tasks that could be automated entirely. Gives managers a prioritized list: "This task wastes 12 hours/week across your team. Here's how to eliminate it."

Real coaching examples

These are the kinds of nudges your team receives — specific, timely, and based on their actual work.

Data entry · Sent to worker
"You're manually formatting each row in the export file. Try Ctrl+Shift+L to auto-format the entire column at once. Your colleague Maria does this and saves about 25 minutes per batch."
Project management · Sent to worker
"You spend ~35 minutes every Monday copying data between your tracking sheet and the client report. This can be automated with a simple formula link. Want me to show you how?"
Customer support · Sent to worker
"You're switching between your CRM and email about 40 times per hour on ticket responses. Using the CRM's built-in email panel would cut that in half and keep your context in one place."
Automation opportunity · Sent to manager
"Your team spends a combined 18 hours/week on invoice reconciliation across 4 people. Based on the process pattern, ~80% of this could be automated with a simple integration between your accounting tool and CRM."
