What to expect

Every team is different, but here's the pattern we see consistently.

Week 1

Install and observe

Go2's desktop agent is installed on your team's machines (takes about 5 minutes each). The first week is pure observation. No coaching yet — the AI is learning how your team works. Your team barely notices it's there.

Weeks 2-3

First coaching and quick wins

Workers start receiving their first personalized tips. These are usually the low-hanging fruit: shortcuts they didn't know, features they were overlooking, small workflow adjustments. Most teams see a 5-10% efficiency bump in this phase alone.

Month 1

Manager insights and automation mapping

You receive your first team-level report. This is where the bigger picture emerges: where time goes across your organization, which processes have the most waste, and a prioritized list of automation opportunities with estimated ROI.

Month 2-3

Deep optimization

With a full picture of your team's workflows, the AI coaching gets more sophisticated. It starts identifying cross-team patterns, suggesting process changes, and helping workers build on each other's best practices. Most teams see 15-25% productivity improvement by this point.

Quarter 1 complete

Structural changes

By now, you've implemented the top automation opportunities and your team has internalized the coaching. The gains compound: better processes, better-trained people, less waste. Teams typically report that Go2 has "paid for itself many times over" by this point.
