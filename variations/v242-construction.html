Options: (1) Shift electrical crew to Building A first (framing complete), (2) Add 2 framers for weekend work ($3,200, recovers 2 days). Option 1 avoids cost and keeps electrical crew productive. Notify electrical sub now.
Subcontractor Communication -- Change Order
Plumbing sub submitted change order for $14,000 citing "unforeseen conditions."
Go2 Coach: Review the original scope: Section 3.2 of the plumbing sub-contract covers "existing conditions as shown in survey." The soil survey from Feb 2025 shows rock at 4' depth in this area. This was foreseeable. Counter-offer: $4,200 for additional labor to cut rock, which is the actual extra work beyond spec.
Weather Delay -- Documentation
Rain delay on concrete pour, need to document for schedule claim.
Go2 Coach: Documenting weather delay: recorded 1.2" rainfall from 6am-2pm. Per contract Section 8.4, delays exceeding 0.5" qualify for schedule extension. Auto-generated delay notice with: weather data, affected activities (foundation pour, grading), crew standby costs ($4,800), and photos. Submit to owner within 48-hr contractual window.
