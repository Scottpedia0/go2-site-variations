How It Works

01

A lightweight desktop agent sits alongside every worker. It captures workflows, processes, and patterns -- not keystrokes. Think of it as institutional memory that never forgets.

Workers get helpful nudges, auto-completed documentation, and personalized tips. The system learns what good looks like and gently guides everyone there.
