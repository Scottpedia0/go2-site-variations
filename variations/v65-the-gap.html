FAQ

Common questions

Isn't this just employee monitoring?
No. Go2 is a coaching platform. Employees get private coaching to help them work better. Managers only see team-level aggregated data, never individual details. No screenshots, no keystroke logging. The goal is to remove friction, not surveil people.
How does the ~1M worker dataset help my team?
When your support rep gets a coaching nudge, it's based on what the best support reps across hundreds of companies actually do. Not theory. Not generic advice. Proven patterns from nearly a million real workers.
What if my employees push back?
Most employees love it. They get direct coaching that helps them work more efficiently, reduce frustration, and leave fewer hours feeling "busy but unproductive." It's like having a personal productivity coach powered by the world's best data.
How long until I see the gap close?
Most teams see measurable improvement within 2-4 weeks. Full gap closure -- the 30%+ efficiency gain -- typically materializes over 90 days as habits shift and automation gets implemented.
What AI models power the coaching?
Go2 uses frontier AI from Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT), and Google (Gemini). These models analyze workflow patterns against our dataset and generate personalized, role-specific coaching for each employee.
