Go2 Sales Coaching Playbook

Real-time call coaching ▼


Go2 listens to call patterns (not content) and provides contextual nudges: talk-to-listen balance, question frequency, engagement signals. Not recording — just pattern matching against your top performers.

Follow-up automation ▼


Go2 detects when a rep hasn't followed up on a hot lead and sends a coaching nudge with a suggested message template based on the prospect's engagement signals.

Pipeline coaching ▼


Daily pipeline digest highlighting deals at risk, stale opportunities, and optimal next actions — all based on patterns from your top performers' behavior.

Objection handling training ▼


Go2 identifies common objections your team faces and shows how top performers handle them. Auto-generates objection response guides from real winning patterns.

New rep acceleration ▼


New reps get a personalized coaching path based on top performer workflows. Week 1: shadow mode. Week 2-4: guided selling with real-time coaching. Week 5+: independent with periodic check-ins.
