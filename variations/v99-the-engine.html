Common Questions

Under the hood

Is this employee surveillance?
No. Go2 captures work patterns, not personal activity. Individual employees get private AI coaching. Managers only see aggregated team insights — never individual monitoring data. We built this to help workers, not watch them.
How does the AI coaching work?
Go2 uses frontier AI models (Claude, GPT, Gemini) to analyze work patterns across ~1M workers. It gives each employee personalized suggestions: faster workflows, shortcuts they're missing, and techniques used by top performers in similar roles. Coaching is private to each employee.
What does the desktop agent actually capture?
Tool usage patterns, process flows, and collaboration dynamics. Not keystrokes, not screenshots, not personal browsing. Think of it as a fitness tracker for work — it tracks the movement, not the conversation.
How long until we see results?
Most teams see measurable improvements within 30 days. Full optimization typically happens within 90 days. The AI gets smarter the longer it observes, so results compound over time.
Will my team resist this?
Teams typically embrace Go2 because it helps them personally. The AI coach makes their work easier, not harder. When employees see it's helping them — not monitoring them — adoption is organic.
