Interpretation notes

Recovered labor value is the largest driver. To remain finance-rigorous, the model multiplies time saved by a value capture rate rather than assuming 100% monetization.
Meeting savings are separated from broader productivity recovery so finance teams can choose whether to treat them as incremental or overlapping. This model treats them as additive but visible.
Admin time is costed using the same average hourly loaded rate to avoid understating internal effort.
Go2 is positioned as AI productivity coaching — insight into work patterns that supports smarter workflows, improved habits, and management coaching, rather than employee surveillance.

Recommended CFO sensitivity bands

Conservative: 2–4 hours saved per week, 25–40% capture rate, 5–10% meeting efficiency gain.
Expected: 4–8 hours saved per week, 40–65% capture rate, 10–15% meeting efficiency gain.
Aggressive: 8–12 hours saved per week, 65–85% capture rate, 15–20% meeting efficiency gain.
Benchmark anchor: Go2 cites 42% average productivity increase, 15.2 hours saved/week/employee, and 892% ROI in year one. Use this as an upside case, not a base case, for board materials.
