Compliance FAQ

Questions Your Legal Team Will Ask

We've pre-answered them all.

Does Go2 capture any PHI (Protected Health Information)?+
No. Go2 captures aggregate work patterns only: time allocation, tool usage categories, collaboration frequency. We never capture screen content, keystrokes, file contents, or any data that could contain PHI. Our architecture makes PHI capture technically impossible.
Where is employee data stored?+
You choose: US (AWS us-east-1), EU (AWS eu-west-1), or Australia (AWS ap-southeast-2). Data never leaves your chosen region. We provide written documentation of data residency for your compliance files.
Can employees request deletion of their data?+
Yes. Full GDPR right-to-erasure support. Employee data can be completely deleted within 24 hours of request. We also support automated deletion policies configurable by your admin.
Do you have a BAA (Business Associate Agreement)?+
Yes. BAAs are available on all plans for healthcare customers. Our standard BAA has been approved by legal teams at 50+ healthcare organizations. Custom BAAs available on Enterprise.
How does Go2 handle employee consent?+
Go2 provides configurable consent workflows. Employees can be required to acknowledge and consent before data collection begins. All consent records are logged and auditable. We support opt-in, opt-out, and informed consent models per jurisdiction.
Can we get your SOC 2 report?+
Yes. Our SOC 2 Type II report is available under NDA. Request it through your account manager or email compliance@go2.io. We also provide a compliance package that includes our ISO 27001 certificate, penetration test summary, and security architecture documentation.
