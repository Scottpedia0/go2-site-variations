Chapter Two

The First Insight

Go2's AI analyzed two weeks of work patterns and delivered its first insights report. The findings were uncomfortable. The support team — 8 people handling 600+ tickets per day — was spending nearly half their time on something nobody had noticed.

"The AI found that our support team was manually copying order details between 3 systems — 23 times per day, per person. That's 2,300 copy-pastes per week."
— Marcus Webb, Support Team Lead

Each copy-paste took about 45 seconds: find the order in Shopify, copy the details, paste into the fulfillment system, then paste again into the CRM. Multiply that by 8 people, 5 days a week. The math was staggering.
