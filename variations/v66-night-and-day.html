FAQ

Common questions

Is this employee surveillance?
No. Go2 coaches employees privately and gives managers only aggregated team data. No screenshots, no keystrokes, no individual browsing data. It's designed to help people, not watch them.
How is this different from time tracking software?
Time trackers tell you THAT people worked 8 hours. Go2 tells you WHERE those hours went, WHAT could be automated, and HOW each person can improve -- using AI coaching trained on nearly a million workers.
Will employees feel monitored?
Employees receive direct coaching that helps them work better. Most appreciate it -- it's like having a personal productivity advisor. Managers never see individual data, only team trends.
How fast does it work?
First insights within 48 hours. Coaching starts in week 1. Measurable team improvement in 2-4 weeks. Full ROI within 90 days.
What size team is this for?
Designed for SMBs with 15-50 remote employees. The aggregated insights work best with 15+ team members, and the per-employee coaching works at any team size.
What AI powers Go2?
Frontier models from Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT), and Google (Gemini) analyze your team's patterns against our ~1M worker dataset to deliver personalized, role-specific coaching.
