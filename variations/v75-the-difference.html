Common questions

Do employees know Go2 is running?

Yes, always. Go2 is visible on the desktop. Workers see their own coaching tips in real-time. Transparency is a feature, not a bug — teams that know Go2 is helping them adopt coaching tips 3x faster than those who discover it later.

How is this different from Time Doctor, Hubstaff, or Teramind?

Those tools monitor. Go2 coaches. They track activity to catch slackers. We analyze workflows to make everyone better. They show managers who's working. We show teams how to work smarter. Fundamentally different philosophy, fundamentally different product.

What kind of companies see the biggest difference?

SMBs with 15-50 remote employees who rely heavily on SaaS tools (CRM, project management, spreadsheets, email). The more repetitive computer-based work your team does, the more hours Go2 recovers. Service businesses, agencies, ecommerce operations, and logistics companies see results fastest.

What happens to my data?

Your workflow data trains YOUR AI models. It never leaves your organization, is never shared with other companies, and is never used to train general-purpose AI. You own your data completely. Period.
