Common questions

Does it slow down people's computers?
No. The agent uses less than 1% CPU and minimal memory. It's lighter than most browser extensions. Workers won't notice it's running.
What if my team pushes back?
We've designed Go2 so workers actually want it. They get a personal AI coach that makes them better at their job. 89% of workers in our data say the coaching tips are useful. We also provide a ready-made page you can share with your team before rollout. See it here.
Can managers see individual screens or activity?
No. Never. Managers see team-level aggregates: workflow patterns, bottleneck alerts, automation opportunities. They cannot drill down to individual activity. This isn't configurable — it's the architecture.
How is this different from Time Doctor, Hubstaff, or Teramind?
Those tools monitor and report. Go2 coaches and improves. They take screenshots and count keystrokes. Go2 uses frontier AI (Claude, GPT, Gemini) to understand workflows and help every person work smarter. It's the difference between a surveillance camera and a personal trainer.
What size team does this work for?
Go2 is built for teams of 15-500 people. It works best when there's enough team data to find shared patterns, but small enough that you can actually act on insights.
How fast do we see results?
First coaching tips arrive within 4 hours. Manager dashboard populates by day 3. Automation recommendations by end of week 1. Measurable team-wide improvement typically by week 3-4.
What data do you collect?
The agent observes application usage, workflow patterns, tool switching, and task sequences. It does NOT capture passwords, personal messages, or financial information. Workers have full visibility into their own data at all times.
