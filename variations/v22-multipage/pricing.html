FAQ

Common questions

What's included in the free trial?

Full access to your selected plan for 14 days. No credit card required. You'll see results within the first week — most teams identify 5+ hours of recoverable time per person.

Can I start with Team and upgrade later?

Absolutely. Most customers start with Team to see their workflow data, then upgrade to Business when they're ready to deploy AI agents. Your data carries over seamlessly.

How many agents are included in Business?

Business includes up to 5 custom AI agent deployments per team. Additional agents can be added for $200/month each. Enterprise includes unlimited agents.

Is there a minimum team size?

Team plan requires a minimum of 5 users. Business requires 10 users. No maximum. Enterprise pricing is available for 100+ users.

What about employee privacy?

Workers see all their own data. Managers only see aggregated team insights — never individual keystrokes or screenshots. Employees can pause capture at any time. Full details on our How It Works page.

Do you offer annual discounts?

Yes. Annual billing saves ~17% compared to monthly. Team drops from $35 to $29/user/month. Business drops from $59 to $49/user/month.

What systems do your agents integrate with?

Our agents work with any tool that has an API — Slack, Salesforce, HubSpot, Jira, Google Workspace, Microsoft 365, Zendesk, and hundreds more. Custom integrations available on Business and Enterprise.

Can I cancel anytime?

Monthly plans can be cancelled at any time. Annual plans can be cancelled with 30 days notice. We'll export your data in any format you need.
