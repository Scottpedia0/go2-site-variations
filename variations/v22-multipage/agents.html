The Path

From observation to automation

Every AI agent deployment follows the same proven path. No guesswork.

Week 1-2

Capture & Observe

Go2 captures activity across your team — apps, workflows, patterns, repetitions. AI begins identifying processes that are performed repeatedly across multiple people.

Week 2-3

Map & Score

Workflows are mapped step-by-step and scored for automation potential. Go2 identifies which processes are high-frequency, rule-based, and cross-application — the ideal candidates for AI agents.

Week 3-4

Design & Build

Our automation engineers design AI agents tailored to your specific workflows. Not generic bots — agents trained on how your team actually works, using the tools you already have.

Week 4-5

Deploy & Monitor

Agents are deployed alongside your team. They start in supervised mode — humans verify outputs before agents run autonomously. Go2 continuously monitors performance and accuracy.

Ongoing

Optimize & Expand

As workflows evolve, agents adapt. Go2 continuously discovers new automation opportunities and deploys additional agents. The system gets smarter the longer it runs.
