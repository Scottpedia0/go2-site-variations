Coaching is powered by frontier AI models (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) that analyze individual patterns and deliver specific, actionable suggestions. Not generic tips. Personalized guidance based on each person's actual work data.

  • Real-time nudges when unproductive patterns are detected
  • Daily briefings with personalized productivity tips
  • Workflow shortcuts discovered from high performers
  • Focus protection suggestions based on peak hours
  • Tool and process recommendations tailored to role

Workflow Mapping

Go2 doesn't just track time — it maps the actual sequences of actions your team performs. Every process becomes visible: the steps, the tools, the handoffs, the bottlenecks.

This is the foundation for automation. Before you can deploy AI agents, you need to know exactly what they'll be doing. Go2 builds that map automatically.

  • Automatic process discovery across applications
  • Step-by-step workflow documentation
  • Variation detection (different people, different approaches)
  • Automation readiness scoring for each workflow
  • Direct handoff to AI agent deployment

Privacy

Built for trust, not surveillance

Every employee has full access to their own activity data and coaching insights. Nothing is hidden. Complete transparency about what's captured and why.

📊

Managers Get Aggregates

Managers never see individual keystrokes, screenshots, or raw activity logs. They get team-level patterns, trends, and recommendations — the signal, not the surveillance.
