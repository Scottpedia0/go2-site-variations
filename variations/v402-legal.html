The Problem

Law Firms Leak Revenue Every Day

The billable hour model is broken. Not because of the concept, but because of the execution. Here's what's costing your firm millions.

⏱

Lost Billable Time

Attorneys lose an average of 40% of billable time to administrative tasks, context switching, and untracked work. That's $200K+ per attorney per year walking out the door.

40% of attorney time is non-billable (Thomson Reuters, 2025)
📄

Document Review Bottlenecks

Manual document review remains the single largest cost in litigation. Associates spend 60% of their time on review tasks that AI can accelerate by 3x or more.

$2.5B spent annually on document review in US litigation
👨‍🎓

Associate Development Gaps

First-year associates take 3-5 years to become consistently profitable. Without structured coaching, firms lose 38% of associates within 3 years — taking institutional knowledge with them.

38% associate attrition within 3 years (NALP, 2025)
📈

No Workflow Visibility

Managing partners make staffing decisions with incomplete data. Which matters are overstaffed? Which associates need mentoring? Without visibility, you're guessing.

67% of managing partners lack real-time productivity data
