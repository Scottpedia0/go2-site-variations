Under The Hood

Skills are code. Open, portable, yours.

Every Go2 skill is a structured definition that any AI platform can consume. No lock-in. No proprietary formats.
  skills/proposal-generation.yaml
name: "proposal-generation"
version: 2.4
trained_from: 847 # observed workflows

context:
  templates: "./templates/proposals/"
  pricing: "./sops/pricing-v3.md"
  tone: "./brand/voice-guide.md"
  crm: "hubspot"

tools:
  - name: "get_client_data"
    source: "hubspot"
    params: ["client_id"]
  - name: "select_template"
    logic: "match tier + industry"
  - name: "apply_pricing"
    sop: "pricing-v3"

deploy:
  claude: "mcp-tools + knowledge"
  chatgpt: "custom-gpt + actions"
  openai: "function-calling"
