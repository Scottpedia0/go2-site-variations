How it works

Observe. Understand. Operate. Automate.

Go2 sits beneath your entire stack. A lightweight agent captures work activity, and frontier AI transforms it into organizational intelligence.

01

Capture the real work

A lightweight desktop agent observes screen activity, keystrokes, and application context across every tool your team uses. No manual logging. No integrations to configure. It just watches.

02

Build organizational memory

Frontier AI (Claude, GPT-4, Gemini) processes raw activity into structured understanding. How does your team actually work? What patterns emerge? What workflows exist that nobody documented?

03

Coach every individual

Each team member gets a personal AI coach with deep context on their work patterns. Not surveillance. Genuine productivity coaching — like having a brilliant mentor watching over your shoulder, suggesting better paths.

04

Generate SOPs and playbooks

Go2 watches your best people work and automatically documents their processes. New hire onboarding goes from months to days. Tribal knowledge becomes institutional knowledge.

05

Deploy AI agents

Once Go2 understands a workflow deeply enough, it can run it. AI agents handle repetitive processes end-to-end, working across your tools exactly like your best people do.
