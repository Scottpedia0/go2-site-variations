FAQ

Common Questions About the Audit

What exactly does the agent capture?▼
Go2's desktop agent captures keystrokes, screenshots, audio, active applications, and workflow patterns. All data is processed by AI to generate insights — managers see aggregated team-level data, never raw surveillance feeds. Individual employees receive personalized coaching based on their own patterns.
Is this employee surveillance?▼
No. Go2 is fundamentally different from surveillance tools. Managers get aggregated insights about team patterns, bottlenecks, and opportunities — not keystroke logs or screenshot galleries. Employees get personalized AI coaching. The goal is improvement, not monitoring.
What AI models power the analysis?▼
Go2 uses frontier AI models including Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), and Gemini (Google). This multi-model approach ensures the highest quality analysis and coaching. No other workforce tool uses generative AI at this level.
How is the audit actually free?▼
The audit is genuinely free with no strings attached. We know that once you see the insights Go2 produces, most companies want to continue with ongoing AI coaching. But you're under no obligation — take your audit report and go.
How long does installation take?▼
The desktop agent installs in under 5 minutes per machine. No IT infrastructure changes required. Most teams of 15-50 people are fully deployed within an hour.
What if my team is concerned about privacy?▼
Transparency is key. We recommend telling your team that Go2 is being used for an operations audit. Employees benefit directly through personalized AI coaching. Their individual data is never shown to managers — only aggregated team patterns.
