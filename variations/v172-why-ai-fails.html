Chapter II

The anatomy of AI failure

It follows a predictable pattern. We've studied hundreds of failed deployments. The path is almost always the same.

01

The Excitement Phase

Leadership sees a demo. "This will transform our business." Budget is allocated. A team is assembled. An AI vendor is selected. Everyone is optimistic.

02

The Data Request

The AI team asks: "Where is your process documentation? Your workflow data? Your operational patterns?" Silence. Nobody has it. It doesn't exist in structured form.

03

The Manual Sprint

Teams spend months doing interviews, surveys, workshops. Trying to manually document what people do. The resulting docs are incomplete, outdated by the time they're finished, and biased by self-reporting.

04

The Quiet Death

The AI pilot produces generic outputs. No one trusts it. Adoption stalls. The project is "deprioritized." Another failed AI initiative. The budget is gone. The team is demoralized.
