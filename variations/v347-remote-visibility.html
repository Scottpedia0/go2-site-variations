🔒 No Screenshots

Go2 never captures your screen. Period. We analyze work patterns, not screen content.

🔒 No Keylogging

We don't count keystrokes or track typing patterns. That's surveillance, not coaching.

🔒 Aggregated Data Only

Managers see team-level patterns, not individual activity. Individual data stays private to the employee.

🔒 Employee-Controlled Sharing

Employees choose what insights they share with managers. Transparency by choice, not force.

🔒 No "Active/Idle" Status

Taking a walk to think through a problem? That's productive. We don't penalize human work patterns.

🔒 Coaching, Not Scoring

No "productivity scores." No leaderboards. Just personalized coaching to help everyone do their best work.
