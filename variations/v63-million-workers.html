FAQ

Common questions

Is this employee surveillance?
No. Go2 is a coaching tool, not a surveillance tool. Employees get direct, personalized coaching. Managers only see aggregated team-level insights -- never individual screenshots, keystrokes, or browsing history. The goal is to help people work better, not watch them.
What makes the ~1M worker dataset valuable?
Every other tool starts from scratch with your team. Go2 already knows what "great" looks like for virtually every knowledge work role. Day one, your team gets benchmarked against the best. No ramp-up period, no guessing.
Which AI models does Go2 use?
Go2 uses frontier AI from Claude (Anthropic), GPT (OpenAI), and Gemini (Google). These models analyze patterns across the dataset and deliver coaching that's specific to each person's role and workflow. No generic advice.
How long until I see results?
Most teams see measurable improvements within 2-4 weeks. The AI starts delivering coaching within the first 48 hours of data collection. Full ROI typically materializes within 90 days.
What size teams does Go2 work best for?
Go2 is designed for small and mid-size businesses, typically 15-50 remote employees. The aggregated insights become most valuable at around 15 people, and the coaching works for any team size.
Is employee data safe?
Absolutely. All data is encrypted in transit and at rest. Individual coaching data is only visible to the employee themselves. Managers never see individual-level data. Go2 is designed with privacy at its core.

Raw data gets mapped against our taxonomy of 8,400+ workflow patterns across 142 industries. A "customer ticket resolution" workflow in a SaaS company gets compared to similar resolution patterns in healthcare, finance, and logistics -- revealing universal efficiency principles.

Claude, GPT, and Gemini each analyze the patterns independently, then Go2 synthesizes their insights. This multi-model approach catches patterns any single AI might miss. The result: coaching that's more accurate and more nuanced than anything a human productivity consultant could deliver.
