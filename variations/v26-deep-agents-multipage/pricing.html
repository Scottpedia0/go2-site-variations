Questions
Frequently asked

Can I start with Observe and upgrade later?

Yes. Most customers start with Observe, let it collect data for 2-4 weeks, then upgrade to Coach once they see the workflow maps. The data you've already collected carries forward — no restart needed.

What's the minimum team size?

Observe and Coach work with any team size. Automate requires a minimum of 25 users because the AI agent quality depends on observing enough workflow variants across your team.

How long is the free trial?

14 days for Observe and Coach. For Automate, we run a 30-day pilot program with dedicated onboarding support. You'll see your first automation candidates within the first two weeks.

Do you offer volume discounts?

Yes. Teams over 100 users get custom pricing. We also offer discounted annual commitments. Contact sales for a quote tailored to your organization.

What if workers don't want to be observed?

We provide rollout communication templates and worker consent flows. Workers can pause observation at any time. In practice, once workers see the coaching benefits and understand the privacy architecture, adoption rates exceed 90%.

What does "open-source agent code access" mean?

On the Automate tier, you get full source code for your deployed agents. They're built on open-source frameworks (CrewAI, AutoGen, LangGraph). You can inspect, modify, and run them on your own infrastructure if you choose.

Is my data used to train models for other customers?

Never. Your workflow data is isolated to your organization. Models trained on your data are yours. We maintain strict data segregation across all customers.

What systems does the desktop agent support?

Windows 10+ and macOS 12+. The agent works with any application — web apps, desktop software, terminal applications. No integrations to configure. If your worker uses it, we can observe it.
