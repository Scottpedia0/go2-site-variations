How It Works

A quiet engine that watches,
learns, and coaches

Go2's desktop agent captures how work happens, coaches workers in real time, and maps every process in your organization. Here's exactly what's happening under the hood.

The Capture Engine
Observing work as it actually happens

A lightweight desktop agent runs in the background. No browser extensions. No API integrations to configure. It sees what your worker sees.

What gets captured

The agent observes application usage, workflow sequences, time spent on tasks, context switches, and interaction patterns. It doesn't record keystrokes or read message content. It understands work at the task and process level.

  • Which applications are used and in what sequence
  • How long each task and sub-task takes
  • Where workers switch between tools or get stuck
  • Patterns of copy-paste, data entry, and form filling
  • Deviations from the most efficient observed paths

How it processes data

Raw observations are processed locally on the worker's machine first. Only structured workflow data leaves the device. Screen content is analyzed for task identification, then discarded. The AI builds a workflow graph, not a surveillance feed.

The lightweight footprint

The agent uses less than 1% CPU and under 80MB of RAM. Workers consistently report forgetting it's there within the first day. It doesn't slow down their machine, doesn't interfere with their applications, and doesn't require any behavior changes.

Two Perspectives
Different views for different roles

Workers and managers see entirely different interfaces. Workers get coaching. Managers get insights. Neither sees the other's view.

💡

Contextual coaching tips

Gentle suggestions when the AI spots a faster approach, based on what top performers do. Workers can dismiss, snooze, or engage with any tip. The AI learns their preferences over time.

📈

Personal productivity dashboard

Their own metrics, trends, and improvements. How their task times compare to their own baseline (not to other workers). Celebrating progress, identifying focus areas.

🎯

Focus mode controls

Workers can activate focus blocks, pause notifications, or tell the coach they're doing deep work. They're in control of when and how the AI interacts with them.

🔒

Privacy controls

Workers can see exactly what data is being captured, pause recording during personal time, and flag activities as private. Full transparency about what the system observes.

📊

Aggregated team insights

Team-level metrics: average task times, workflow efficiency trends, tool adoption rates. No individual worker data unless the worker opts in to share their improvements.

🗺

Process maps

Visual maps of how work flows through the team. Where bottlenecks are. Where different workers take different paths for the same task. Which processes have the most variance.

🤖

Automation opportunities

Ranked list of tasks that are candidates for AI agent deployment. Estimated time savings, implementation complexity, and ROI projections for each one.

📋

Coaching effectiveness

How coaching is impacting team performance. Which tips are being adopted. Where workers are improving fastest. Aggregate engagement without individual tracking.

Privacy Architecture
Built for trust from the ground up

Privacy isn't a feature we bolted on. It's the architecture. Here's how we ensure Go2 is a tool workers welcome, not resist.

💻

Local-first processing

Screen content is analyzed on the worker's device. Only structured workflow data (app names, task types, timing) leaves the machine. No screenshots stored. No content transmitted.

🛡

Aggregate-only manager views

Managers see team-level data. They cannot drill down to individual workers' screens, activities, or browsing. Individual data stays with the individual.

⏸

Worker pause controls

Workers can pause observation at any time. Personal time, sensitive work, private browsing — they control when the agent is active. No justification required.

🔐

End-to-end encryption

All data in transit and at rest is encrypted. SOC 2 Type II compliant. GDPR and CCPA ready. Your data never trains models for other customers.

📝

Data retention you control

Set retention periods per data type. Auto-delete after 30, 60, or 90 days. Export or purge individual worker data at any time. Your data, your rules.

✅

Consent-first rollout

Workers see exactly what Go2 does before they start. No hidden monitoring. Full opt-in transparency. We provide rollout communication templates to make the conversation easy.
