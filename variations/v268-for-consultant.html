01
Value Stream Mapping
Process efficiency analysis
Consultant: 6 weeks | Go2: Automatic

Traditional VSM requires workshops, sticky notes, and weeks of facilitation. Go2 generates value stream maps automatically by observing actual work patterns. Every process variant is captured. Lead times, cycle times, and wait times are measured precisely, not estimated.

02
Time & Motion Study
Work allocation analysis
Consultant: 4 weeks | Go2: Real-time

Consultants shadow employees for days, introducing Hawthorne effect bias. Go2 captures time allocation passively and continuously. No behavior changes from being observed. Complete data set, not a 3-day sample. Breakdowns by tool, process, communication, and focus time.

03
Organizational Network Analysis
Collaboration & communication patterns
Consultant: 3 weeks | Go2: Continuous

Consultants use surveys to map informal networks. Response rates are 40-60%. Go2 maps actual collaboration patterns from work data. Identifies bottleneck individuals, isolated teams, and communication silos. Updated daily, not once per engagement.

04
Capability Maturity Assessment
Process maturity scoring
Consultant: 4 weeks | Go2: Auto-scored

CMMI assessments require trained assessors and weeks of evaluation. Go2 auto-scores process maturity based on standardization, documentation, measurement, and optimization criteria. Tracks maturity progression over time. Identifies quick wins for maturity improvement.
