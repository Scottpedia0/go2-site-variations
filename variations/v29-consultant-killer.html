No. Go2 is an operations intelligence platform. It analyzes workflow patterns and tool usage to find inefficiencies — just like a consultant would. It does not record screens, log keystrokes, or track individual performance for surveillance purposes. The goal is coaching, not policing.

Time trackers tell you where hours went. Go2 tells you why those hours were wasted and fixes the root cause. Hubstaff shows you someone spent 3 hours in spreadsheets. Go2 shows you the spreadsheet workflow is redundant and automates it away.
