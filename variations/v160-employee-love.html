Honest Answers

Questions employees ask (and deserve answers to)

"Is this just surveillance with a nicer name?"
No. Go2 does not take screenshots, log keystrokes, or monitor your screen. It analyzes workflow patterns -- tool switching, process steps, time allocation -- to help YOU work better. Your manager sees aggregate team metrics, not your individual activity.
"Can my manager see exactly what I am doing?"
No. Managers see team-level insights: where processes break down, where onboarding gaps exist, which tools cause friction. They cannot see your individual screen, your messages, or your minute-by-minute activity. Your personal coaching data is private to you.
"Will this be used against me in reviews?"
Go2 does not generate individual performance scores or rankings. It helps YOU improve. The coaching is between you and the AI. Teams that use Go2 report better reviews because everyone performs better -- not because anyone is being watched.
"What if I do not want to use it?"
We get it. Every company deploys Go2 differently, but our data shows something interesting: 89% of employees who try it for 2 weeks ask to keep it. The value is that clear. Give it a real shot.
"Does it slow down my computer?"
The desktop agent uses less than 50MB of RAM and near-zero CPU. It runs lighter than most browser tabs. You genuinely will not notice it.
