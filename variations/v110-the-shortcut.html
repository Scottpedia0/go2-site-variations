Common Questions

Straight answers only.

No marketing spin. If the answer is "no," we'll say so.

Will my employees feel like they're being watched?

No. Go2 doesn't take screenshots, log keystrokes, or track individual browsing. It observes work patterns at a high level — which tools are used, how workflows flow, where time goes. Workers get AI coaching from it. Managers see aggregated team data, never individual surveillance. Most teams report that it feels empowering, not intrusive.

How long until we see results?

The desktop agent starts capturing patterns immediately. Within one to two weeks, AI coaching begins flowing to workers and manager dashboards populate. Automation mapping typically surfaces high-value opportunities within the first month. This isn't a six-month implementation — it's a shortcut, remember?

What makes the AI coaching better than generic advice?

Go2 uses frontier AI models — Claude, GPT, and Gemini — analyzing your team's actual work data against patterns from nearly a million workers. The coaching is specific to each person's role, tools, and workflows. It's not "drink more water and take breaks." It's "here's how the most effective people in your role handle this specific workflow."

What size company is this for?

Go2 is designed for small to mid-size businesses with 15 to 50 remote employees. Big enough to have real workflow complexity and management challenges. Small enough that every efficiency gain is felt directly on the bottom line. If you're in that range and managing a remote team, this is built for you.

Does my team need to change how they work?

No. Zero behavior change required to start. The agent observes silently. When coaching arrives, workers can take it or leave it. Over time, most do adopt the suggestions because they're practical and specific. But there's no disruption on day one — or any day.

How is this different from Time Doctor, Hubstaff, etc.?

Those are monitoring tools — they track activity to prove people are working. Go2 is a productivity tool — it uses AI to help people work better. We don't take screenshots. We don't score "activity." We coach workers, give managers team-level insights, and map automation. Fundamentally different purpose, fundamentally different approach.
