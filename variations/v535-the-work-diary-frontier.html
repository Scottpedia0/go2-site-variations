What the week taught me

“I don’t need more effort. I need better patterns.”
🖋️

The surprising part wasn’t how hard I worked. It was how visible the pattern became once someone — or something — reflected it back clearly. My best hours weren’t random. My worst days weren’t personal failures. They were design flaws in the way my workday was structured.
