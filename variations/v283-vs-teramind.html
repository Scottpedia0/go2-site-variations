The Philosophical Divide

Teramind assumes the worst about employees. Go2 brings out their best.

Teramind
Assumption: Employees are a risk to mitigate.
Data Loss Prevention (DLP)
Blocks file transfers, monitors email content, prevents data exfiltration. Built for security teams.
Keystroke Logging
Records every keystroke. Yes, every single one. Including passwords in some configurations.
Screen Recording
Continuous or triggered video recording of employee screens. Full surveillance.
Behavioral Analytics
Machine learning to detect "anomalous behavior" — employees who deviate from normal patterns.
Content Inspection
Reads email content, chat messages, file contents. Scans for policy violations.
Insider Threat Detection
Treats every employee as a potential insider threat until proven otherwise.
VS
Go2
Assumption: Employees are potential to unlock.
AI Workflow Coaching
Learns how top performers work and coaches everyone to that level. Personalized, proactive guidance.
Automation Discovery
Identifies repetitive patterns and builds automations. Eliminates drudgework, keeps the interesting stuff.
Auto-Documentation
Captures workflows as living, breathing documentation. SOPs write themselves and stay current.
Intelligence Analytics
ML to find optimization opportunities — workflows that could be 10x faster, tools that aren't being utilized.
AI Sidecar
Gives ChatGPT/Claude company-specific context so AI answers are actually relevant. Not generic.
Agent Training
Learns from human workflows to train AI agents that handle routine tasks autonomously.

Same Situation. Opposite Responses.

How each tool reacts to common workplace events.

📧
Employee Emails a Large File
Teramind: Alert! Potential data exfiltration. Block. Notify security. Review content. Interview employee.
Go2: "You send large files often. Want me to set up a shared workspace so recipients can access them instantly?"
🔄
Employee Does Same Task 50x
Teramind: Logs it as "normal behavior pattern." No insight. No action. Just more surveillance data.
Go2: "This task is repeatable. I can automate it. You'll save 8 hours/week. Want me to set it up?"
🌐
Employee Visits Competitor Website
Teramind: Red flag alert. Possible job search or corporate espionage. Escalate to management immediately.
Go2: "I see you're researching competitors. Here's an auto-generated competitive analysis based on your company's data."
💬
Employee Uses ChatGPT
Teramind: Potential data leak! Content inspection triggered. May block the tool entirely.
Go2: Sidecars the AI with company context. Employee gets 10x better answers. Knowledge is captured, not leaked.
🚪
Top Employee Gives Notice
Teramind: Activate departure monitoring. Lock down file access. Screen record everything. Forensic review.
Go2: Their workflows, knowledge, and processes are already documented and training AI agents. Zero knowledge loss.
📊
Manager Wants Team Insights
Teramind: Here's who's violating policies, who's a risk, and who's browsing non-work sites. Fear-based report.
Go2: Here's where your team can be more efficient, which processes need automation, and coaching opportunities.
