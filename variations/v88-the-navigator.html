Route: Manual Data Entry Bypass

Go2 detected an accounts payable clerk entering the same vendor data into two separate systems every day. The navigator identified a CSV import feature in the second system that auto-syncs with the first. One-time 10-minute setup eliminated 45 minutes of daily double-entry.

Time reclaimed: 3.75 hours/week per person
▸

Route: Meeting Preparation Shortcut

A project manager spent 25 minutes before every client call manually pulling status updates from three project boards. Go2 showed them how to create a single dashboard view that auto-aggregates status across all boards, reducing prep to under 3 minutes.

Time reclaimed: 2+ hours/week for someone with 6 weekly client calls
▸

Route: Email Template Discovery

A sales team was individually crafting proposal follow-up emails from scratch. Go2's navigator noticed 80% of the content was identical across reps and coached each person on creating shared templates. The team now sends personalized follow-ups in 2 minutes instead of 15.

Time reclaimed: 1 hour/day across a 5-person sales team
▸

Route: Context Switch Reduction

A customer success manager toggled between their CRM, email, and chat platform over 60 times per day. Go2 identified that most switches were to check for the same information and recommended a unified notification setup. Context switches dropped by 70%, and deep work blocks increased by 40 minutes daily.

Time reclaimed: 40 minutes/day of recovered focus time
