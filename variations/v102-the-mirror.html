Questions

Looking closer

Is this employee surveillance?
Absolutely not. Go2 is designed to help employees, not watch them. Individual coaching is private. Manager dashboards show only aggregated team patterns. We capture work patterns, not personal activity. Think of it as an MRI for your operations, not a security camera for your people.
What if my team doesn't like what the mirror shows?
That's the point. Every company has blind spots. Go2 reveals them so you can fix them. The initial findings can be surprising, but teams consistently tell us that the discomfort of seeing the truth is far outweighed by the satisfaction of improving it.
How does the AI coaching work?
Go2 uses frontier AI models (Claude, GPT, Gemini) to analyze work patterns from ~1M workers. Each employee gets personalized coaching: faster workflows, techniques from top performers, and shortcuts they're missing. All private to the individual.
What does the desktop agent actually capture?
Tool usage patterns, process sequences, and collaboration dynamics. Not keystrokes, not screenshots, not personal browsing. Think fitness tracker for work — it measures the movement, not the conversation.
How long until we see Phase 2 results?
Most teams see the Phase 1 "mirror" insights within the first week. Measurable Phase 2 improvements typically start within 30 days. Full transformation happens within 90 days, and results continue compounding from there.
