Privacy & Trust
Built on trust, not surveillance.
Go2 was designed from day one to earn employee trust. Here's how we do it.

🔒 Employee-Controlled Data

Employees see all their own data. They can pause tracking, exclude apps, and export their data anytime. Full transparency.

🚫 No Keystroke Logging

We never log keystrokes, record screens, or monitor communications content. We analyze work patterns, not surveillance data.

🧠 Aggregate Insights for Managers

Managers see team-level patterns, not individual surveillance. No "who spent how long on what website" dashboards.

🛡 GDPR & Privacy First

Full GDPR, CCPA compliance. Right to deletion. Data minimization. Privacy-by-design architecture. Annual third-party audit.
