Questions & answers

Things you're probably wondering

Won't my employees feel like they're being watched?▼

This is the #1 question we get, and the answer usually surprises people. Within the first week, most employees start asking to keep Go2 — because it's genuinely making their jobs easier. The mentor is fully transparent (employees see what's being observed), and it only gives helpful suggestions, never punitive feedback. It's like having a brilliant senior colleague who shares shortcuts instead of hoarding them.

What exactly does the agent capture?▼

The lightweight desktop agent observes workflow patterns: which applications are used, time spent on tasks, repetitive actions, and workflow sequences. This data feeds the AI models so they can provide specific, contextual coaching. All data is encrypted, and managers only see aggregated team-level insights — never individual activity.

How is this different from employee monitoring software?▼

Traditional monitoring software is built for managers to watch employees. Go2 is built for employees to get better at their jobs. The AI exists to help, not to score or rank. Managers get team-level insights, but never individual surveillance data. Employees actually want to use it because it saves them hours every week.

How fast will we see results?▼

The agent installs in under 5 minutes per machine. Within the first few days, the AI begins surfacing coaching tips. Most teams see measurable productivity improvements within 2-3 weeks. The AI gets smarter over time, so results compound month over month.

What makes the coaching so specific?▼

Go2 is built on 7+ years of workforce data from nearly a million workers. The AI doesn't give generic advice — it sees YOUR employee's actual workflow and compares it against proven patterns from people doing similar work. It's the difference between reading a productivity book and having a coach who watches you work and says "try this instead."

Do you work with teams under 15 or over 50?▼

Our sweet spot is 15-50 remote employees, but we work with teams as small as 10 and as large as 200. The AI coaching works at any scale — the team insights just get richer with more data points.
