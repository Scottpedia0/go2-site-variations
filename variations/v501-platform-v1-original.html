FAQ

Common questions

How is Go2 different from Hubstaff, Time Doctor, or Teramind? +
Those tools capture time and activity — and so does Go2. But Go2 goes further. We take your workforce data and run it through frontier AI models (Claude, GPT, Gemini) trained on patterns from nearly a million remote workers. The AI finds the shortcuts, macros, and automations your team is missing, then proactively delivers them. We also help you implement the changes — configuring your tools' AI features, setting up automations, and optimizing your existing stack. They show you dashboards. We actually fix things.
Will my team feel surveilled? +
Let's be direct: Go2 captures app usage, browser activity, idle time, and workflow patterns. But the raw data goes to AI, not your manager. Workers see their own data and get personalized coaching — shortcuts, automations, AI tools they're missing. Managers get team-level insights and aggregated dashboards, not individual browser histories. There are no screenshots, no keystroke logging, no mouse heatmaps. An AI coach suggesting a faster way to do something is a very different experience than a manager pulling up your screen time report.
What does "frontier AI for every worker" mean? +
Starting at the Perform tier, every team member gets their own work-safe instance of a frontier AI model — Claude, GPT, or Gemini. API calls are included in the subscription. It's like giving everyone their own AI assistant, but with organizational context, shared memory across the team, and all the security and privacy concerns handled. Some workers prefer Claude, some prefer ChatGPT — they all share the same organizational knowledge base.
Do you replace our existing tools? +
Your choice. Go2 can wrap around your existing stack — if you're using Intercom, Zendesk, BambooHR, keep using them. Go2 integrates, captures the data, and helps you get more out of those tools' built-in AI and automation features. Or you can use Go2's built-in alternatives for HR, scheduling, time tracking, and more. Either way, the AI layer works the same.
How long does setup take? +
Most teams are up and running in under an hour. Install the desktop agent, connect your integrations, and Go2 starts capturing immediately. AI guidance recommendations typically begin within the first two weeks as the system identifies patterns. For Enterprise customers, we provide full onboarding and engineering team documentation.
Can I use Go2 just for time tracking? +
Absolutely. The Measure plan gives you everything Hubstaff or Time Doctor does — time tracking, activity capture, scheduling, daily reports, management dashboards. Switching from another platform? Show us your invoice and we'll price match your first year. You can add AI guidance and implementation support later when you're ready.
What's the Enterprise API for? +
Enterprise customers can push all workforce data to their own data store — Snowflake, BigQuery, Redshift, or wherever your team works. Your analysts can query it, your BI tools can visualize it, your engineering team can build on it. Enterprise can be the full Go2 suite or just a background capture agent with an API — your choice. We help your engineering team get set up and provide full documentation.
