Common Questions

Everything you want to know.

How does Go2 learn each employee's work style?

Go2's desktop agent quietly observes how each person works — the tools they use, the patterns they follow, the friction points they encounter. Within 2-3 days, the assistant begins making personalized suggestions. By week two, it feels like it's been there for months. No setup required from the employee beyond installing the lightweight desktop app.

What AI models power the assistant?

Go2 uses frontier AI models from Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT), and Google (Gemini). Each model has different strengths — Go2 automatically routes tasks to the best model for the job. Your team gets world-class AI without needing to know which model is doing what. It just works.

Will my employees actually use it?

This is the number one question we get, and the answer is yes. 92% of employees report that Go2 makes their day measurably easier within the first week. Unlike monitoring tools that employees resent, Go2 is a tool they actually want — because it does work for them, saves them time, and reduces their daily frustrations. Adoption isn't a problem when the tool genuinely helps.

How is this different from ChatGPT or Copilot?

ChatGPT and Copilot are general-purpose tools. Go2's assistant knows your company — your processes, your tools, your team's patterns. It doesn't wait for questions; it proactively handles tasks, prepares your day, and catches things before they become problems. It's the difference between having access to a search engine and having a dedicated executive assistant who knows your business inside and out.

What does the manager/owner see?

Managers receive aggregated team insights: overall capacity, common bottlenecks, tool utilization patterns, and opportunities to help their team work better. They never see individual activity logs, screenshots, or keystrokes. The data is designed to help managers support their teams more effectively, not micromanage them.
