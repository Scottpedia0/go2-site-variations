FAQ

Common Migration Questions

Will my employees notice the switch?+
Most employees prefer Go2 immediately because it's less intrusive. No screenshots, no random popup prompts. The agent runs silently and employees see their own coaching dashboard — which they actually find helpful rather than creepy.
What about my historical data?+
We import up to 2 years of historical data from your current tool. This means your productivity trends, team analytics, and project data carry over seamlessly. No starting from scratch.
Can I run both tools during transition?+
Yes. Go2 runs alongside Time Doctor, Hubstaff, or Teramind without conflicts. We recommend a 3-5 day parallel run for peace of mind, then uninstall the old tool.
How long does migration take?+
Most companies complete migration in under a week. Data import takes 1-2 hours (automated), agent deployment takes 5 minutes per machine, and the optional parallel run is 3-5 days.
What features will I lose?+
If you use screenshots or keystroke logging, those features don't exist in Go2 — by design. We believe surveillance doesn't improve productivity; coaching does. Everything else (time tracking, project analytics, reporting) is available and usually better.
Will my costs change?+
Go2 pricing is $6-12/user/month depending on plan. Most companies switching from Teramind save 30-50% on licensing costs alone, before factoring in the productivity gains.
