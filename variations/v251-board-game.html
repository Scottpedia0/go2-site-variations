How to Play

Objective: Be the first player to reach Square 30: "AI-Powered Organization." You start with $500K in AI budget.

Rolling: Click the dice. You'll move forward 1-6 spaces. Each square triggers a scenario card.

Square Types:

🟢 Go2 squares: Good things happen. You advance, gain budget, or unlock insights.

🔴 Bad AI squares: Classic AI implementation failures. Lose money, move backward.

🟠 Consultant squares: Expensive and slow. You'll pay a lot and gain little.

🔶 Event squares: Random events — could be good or bad.

Bankruptcy: If your budget hits $0, you must restart from Square 1 with $200K (a "pivot").

Winning: First player to land on or pass Square 30 wins!
