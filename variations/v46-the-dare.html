Repeated Manual Tasks

Copy-pasting between apps. Reformatting the same spreadsheet every Monday. Manually updating three systems with the same data. Your team does this dozens of times per day without thinking.

Avg: 3.2 hrs/person/week

Wrong-Tool Usage

Building project trackers in spreadsheets. Using email for instant messaging. Writing formulas that a purpose-built tool handles in one click. People use what they know, not what's best.

Process Bottlenecks

Workflow Redundancy

Two people doing the same task because neither knows the other does it. Reports generated in parallel. Quality checks that duplicate earlier quality checks. Invisible overlap everywhere.
