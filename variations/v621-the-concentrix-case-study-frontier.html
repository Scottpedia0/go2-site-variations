The Business Problem

The issue wasn’t headcount. It was invisible inefficiency.

Concentrix didn’t need another dashboard. It needed a way to understand how work was actually getting done, where friction lived, and how to coach employees in the moment before lost minutes became lost margin.

What leadership saw

Rising labor cost

Productivity variation across teams created preventable waste. Leadership knew there was value trapped inside day-to-day workflows, but lacked a reliable way to diagnose it fast.

What managers lacked

No real context

Traditional analytics showed outputs, not behaviors. They could identify missed targets, but not the exact workflow patterns, app switching, interruptions, or habits causing underperformance.

What employees needed

Personalized coaching

Workers didn’t need surveillance. They needed specific, private, useful guidance that helped them reduce friction, improve focus, and do better work with less stress.

“The breakthrough was not simply seeing work. It was converting work data into coaching employees could actually act on — immediately, repeatedly, and at scale.”
