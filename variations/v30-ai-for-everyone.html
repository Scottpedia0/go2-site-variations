Frequently asked questions

How does Go2 learn about my company? +

A lightweight desktop agent observes how your team uses their tools — which applications, which workflows, which patterns lead to great outcomes. This context feeds into your organization's AI brain. Employees have full control over what's captured, and all data stays within your Go2 instance.

Is this employee surveillance? +

No. Go2 is an AI productivity tool, not a monitoring tool. The desktop agent captures work context to make AI smarter — think of it as teaching AI how your company works. Employees benefit directly through better AI coaching. There are no screenshots, no keystroke logging, no activity scores.

Why multiple AI models instead of just one? +

Different models excel at different tasks. Claude is exceptional at reasoning and analysis. GPT is great at creative generation. Gemini handles multimodal tasks well. Go2 routes to the right model for each task — and all of them benefit from your company's context. You don't need separate subscriptions to each provider.

How is my company's data protected? +

Your organizational data is isolated in your own instance and never used to train foundation models. We use enterprise-grade encryption, SOC 2 compliance (Enterprise tier), and give you full control over data retention. Your competitive intelligence stays yours.

How quickly does the AI learn our workflows? +

Go2 starts providing value from day one with general AI capabilities. Within the first week, it begins understanding your tools and basic workflows. By the end of the first month, it has meaningful organizational context. The AI continuously improves — the longer you use it, the smarter it gets about your business.

What's the free pilot? +

We offer a 14-day free pilot for up to 10 users. You get the full Business tier experience so you can see the real value before committing. No credit card required to start. Most teams see measurable impact within the first two weeks.
