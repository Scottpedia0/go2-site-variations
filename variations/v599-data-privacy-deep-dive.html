2 What We Never Collect

This is not a question of configuration or plan tier. These capabilities do not exist in our codebase. Period.

✗
Screenshots or screen recording

No screen capture capability exists in the agent.

✗
Keystrokes or typing content

No keylogger. We detect keyboard activity (active/idle), not what's typed.

✗
Email, chat, or message content

We see "Slack is open" — never what's being said.

✗
File contents or names

No filesystem access. We don't know what files you open.

✗
Camera or microphone

No media capture APIs. Not present in code, not possible to enable.

✗
GPS or precise location

No location services. Regional data comes from IP at the city level only.

✗
Browser history or bookmarks

Domain-level only when configured. Never full URLs, never browsing history.

✗
Personal app usage during breaks

Configurable break periods exclude all tracking automatically.

3 How Data Is Processed

Your data flows through a defined pipeline. Here's every step, in order:

1

Agent Collection

Desktop agent captures app-level metadata every 30 seconds. Data is encrypted locally before transmission.

↓
2

Encrypted Transit

TLS 1.3 connection to regional API gateway. Certificate pinning prevents MITM attacks. Data never leaves encrypted tunnel.

↓
3

Event Processing

Stream processor validates, deduplicates, and normalizes events. Invalid or suspicious payloads are rejected and logged.

↓
4

AI Classification

ML model categorizes activities (productive, neutral, break) based on app and context. Model runs on our infrastructure — your data never goes to OpenAI, Google, etc.

↓
5

Aggregation

Raw events are aggregated into time blocks (5min, hourly, daily). Individual-level data is available only to that individual and their direct manager (configurable).

↓
6

Insight Generation

AI coaching engine generates personalized recommendations based on patterns. Insights are positive and actionable — never punitive.

↓
7

Dashboard Delivery

Processed data and insights are available via the web dashboard and API. All access is authenticated, authorized, and logged.

5 Who Has Access to Your Data

INDIVIDUAL

The Employee

Full access to their own data, always. Can view, export, or request deletion of their personal productivity data at any time.

MANAGER

Direct Manager

Sees team-level aggregates by default. Individual data visible only if org policy enables it AND the employee is notified.

ADMIN

Org Administrator

Configures policies, manages users, views aggregate org-level analytics. Individual data access requires explicit justification and is audit-logged.

GO2 STAFF

Go2 Engineering

Zero standing access to customer data. Break-glass access requires two-person approval, is time-limited (max 4 hours), and is fully audit-logged.

Third-Party AI Providers

None. Our AI models run entirely on our own infrastructure. Your data is never sent to OpenAI, Google, Anthropic, or any other third-party AI provider. Our models are trained on anonymized, aggregated data only.

7 Your Rights

Regardless of your location, we extend these rights to all Go2 users:

  • ✓
    Right to Access — Download a complete copy of all data we hold about you, in machine-readable JSON format.
  • ✓
    Right to Deletion — Request permanent deletion of all your personal data. Processed within 30 days.
  • ✓
    Right to Correction — Correct any inaccurate data, including activity categorizations.
  • ✓
    Right to Object — Object to specific data processing activities. We'll comply or explain why we can't.
  • ✓
    Right to Portability — Export your data in standard formats (JSON, CSV) at any time.
  • ✓
    Right to Restrict — Pause data collection on your device at any time via the system tray icon.
