You're thinking it. Let's address it.

"Isn't this just employee monitoring in a nicer package?"
No. Monitoring tools watch people and report to managers. Go2 coaches people privately. Managers never see individual data. They see team-level insights. Workers see their own patterns and get personalized tips. That's coaching, not surveillance.
"23% sounds too good to be true."
It does. But consider: the average worker loses 2+ hours daily to context switching, unnecessary meetings, and workflow friction. Recovering even half of that waste is a massive gain. We're not making people work harder — we're removing the things that make them work slower.
"Won't my team resist this?"
94% of workers who use Go2 want to keep using it. Because it helps them, personally. It tells them "you do your best deep work at 10am" and "you lose 45 minutes recovering after your 2pm meeting block." People love data about themselves when it's not used against them.
"We still need to hire for some roles."
Of course. Go2 doesn't replace all hiring. But it does let you hire for growth instead of capacity. Stop hiring to fill gaps. Start hiring to expand into new areas — because your current team is already operating at their potential.
