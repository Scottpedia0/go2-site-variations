Phase 1: AI Coaching
Immediate ROI while we map your workflows

Go2 doesn't just watch. From day one, AI coaching surfaces insights and nudges that make your team faster. This is the foundation for everything that follows.

Capture

Desktop Activity Mapping

Go2 captures screenshots and app usage across every workstation. Not keylogging -- workflow mapping. It sees what tools your team uses, in what order, and for how long.

"Your support team switches between 6 apps per ticket. The top performer uses 3. Here's the difference."
Coach

Real-Time Productivity Nudges

AI watches for patterns: context-switching spirals, time sinks, inefficient sequences. Then it intervenes with specific, actionable guidance. Not surveillance -- coaching.

"You've spent 45 minutes formatting this report. Last week's took 12 minutes. Want to see what changed?"
Insight

Workflow Discovery

Go2 automatically identifies and catalogs every workflow: how invoices get processed, how tickets get resolved, how orders get fulfilled. No interviews needed. No documentation projects.

"We've identified 23 distinct workflows across your team. 8 are candidates for agent automation."
Coach

Best Practice Sharing

When one team member finds a faster way, Go2 notices. It captures the pattern and shares it with the rest of the team. Institutional knowledge spreads automatically.

"Maria processes refunds 3x faster using this sequence. We've created a guide from her workflow."
Insight

Time & Focus Analytics

See where time actually goes, not where people think it goes. App-level breakdowns, meeting load analysis, deep work vs. shallow work ratios. Data your team can act on.

"Your engineering team spends 31% of time in Slack and email. Industry benchmark is 18%."
