FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

Will my employees feel spied on?
No. Go2 is designed as a coaching tool. Employees get direct, private coaching to help them work better. Managers only see team-level aggregated data. No screenshots, no keystroke logging, no individual monitoring. Most employees love it because it actually helps them.
How is this different from a meeting analytics tool?
Meeting analytics tools count your meetings. Go2 finds out why you have so many meetings in the first place -- and it looks at everything else too. Context switching, tool friction, redundant workflows, notification overload. Meetings are just the visible tip.
What does the desktop agent actually capture?
The agent captures work patterns -- application usage, workflow sequences, context switches, and time allocation. It does NOT capture screenshots, keystrokes, or screen content. It sees patterns, not content.
How fast does it work?
First insights within 48 hours. Coaching nudges start within the first week. Measurable team-level improvements typically appear within 2-4 weeks. Full ROI within 90 days.
What size team is this for?
Go2 works best for remote teams of 15-50 people. The aggregated insights become most valuable around 15 team members, and the AI coaching works at any size.
