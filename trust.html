What we capture

Total capture is what makes the AI useful

App usage, browser activity, workflow sequences, keystrokes, audio context, idle time. This is the full picture of how work happens.

This level of capture is what allows Go2 to identify repeated workflows, coaching opportunities, and automation candidates within hours. Most systems capture fragments — Go2 captures the workflow.

This is a technical requirement for AI quality, not a surveillance feature. Partial data produces shallow insights. Complete data produces coaching that actually changes behavior.

FAQ

Common questions about trust and data

Does Go2 capture keystrokes?

Yes. That data feeds AI coaching — it's how Go2 understands actual workflows, not just app switches. Keystroke data never surfaces to managers. Workers see their own data and can delete it anytime.

What do managers actually see?

Aggregated patterns, bottlenecks, workflow opportunities, and recommendations. Team-level signals that help them improve processes — never individual raw activity.

Where is data stored?

In your environment. Go2 supports VPC and on-prem deployment. Your data never leaves your infrastructure.

Can workers delete their data?

Yes. Workers have full data control, including the ability to view everything captured and delete it at any time.

Is Go2 SOC 2 compliant?

Yes. SOC 2 Type II certified with regular third-party audits and enterprise-grade security controls.
